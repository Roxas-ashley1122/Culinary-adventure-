Tom Yum Goong:A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices.This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongras, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp

'The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors — sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.' –Chef Somchai


Ingredients:

  • Lemongrass:2 stalks
  • Thai basil:1 cup
  • Shrimp: 500g

    • Preparation:

    1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
    2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and Kaffir lime leaves.
    3. stir in shrimp andcook until pink.
    4. Season with fish sauce, like juice, and chill paste.
    5. Ganish with Thai basil and serve hot

    Cooking Tips:

    • 1/4 cup2 of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor.
    • 1 tablespoon3 of chili paste gives the soup itsd signature heat.
