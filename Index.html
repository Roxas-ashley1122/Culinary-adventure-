Tom Yum Goong:A spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices.This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir like leaves, combined with succelent shrimp.

"The key to a delicious Tim Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors — sweet, sour, salt and spicy" —Chef Somchai

Ingredients

  • Lemongrass:2 stalks
  • Thai basil:1 cup
  • kaffir lime:3 leaves
  • Shrimp:500g

Preparation

  1. Simmerthe broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stirin shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Seasonwith fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.
  5. Garnishwith Thai basil and serve hot.

Cooking tips

  • 1/4 cupof fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor.
  • 1 tablespoonof chili paste give the soup its signature heat.(spicy serving size)
